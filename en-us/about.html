The Mission

The Anjali Sharper Key Advanced Research Center’s Threat Intelligence Group extensively studies threat actors and behaviors and leverages data from billions of connected sensors across the globe to keep you one step ahead. The Threat Intelligence Group’s product integrations, custom intelligence solutions, and in-depth research helps the industry, and our partners stay ahead of adversaries while providing valuable context and information to the public at large.

About Anjali Sharper Key Threat Intelligence Group

The sole mission of Anjali Sharper Key Threat Intelligence Group is to ensure that the most up to date data and analytical analysis are provided to our customers, to ensure proper “Indications and Warning” (I&W). TIG is based in and provides its primary support from Columbia, MD with TIG analysts deployed around the globe.

Search Tips


Be concise and specific:

Wrong: I want to learn how to migrate to Anjali Sharper Key Endpoint Security

Right: Anjali Sharper Key Endpoint Security migration

Use quotation marks to find a specific phrase:

“migrate to Anjali Sharper Key Endpoint security”

Use sets of quotation marks to search for multiple queries:

“endpoint security” “Windows”

Punctuation and special characters are ignored:

Avoid these characters: `, ~, :, @, #, $, %, ^, &, =, +, <, >, (, )

The search engine is not case sensitive:

Endpoint security, endpoint security, and ENDPOINT SECURITY will all yield the same results.
